Rain Updates

Rain alert in 9 Tamil Nadu districts, Puducherry today; Chennai schools closed...

he northeast monsoon intensified in Tamil Nadu as the India Meteorological Department (IMD) issued rain alerts for several districts in the state and Puducherry's Karaikal. An orange warning has been sounded in Cuddalore, Mayiladhuturai, Nagapattinam and Thiruvarur districts of Tamil Nadu and Karaikal in Puducherry on Wednesday. A yellow alert has been issued for Chennai, Kancheepuram, Tiruvallur, Chengalpattu and Thanjavur districts of Tamil Nadu and Puducherry.The Met Department predicted light to moderate rain along with thunderstorms and lightning in these five districts. Meanwhile, schools in Chennai and educational institutions in Thiruvallur district are shut due to incessant rain. A holiday was also declared for schools and colleges in Puducherry and Karaikal.
